Value Proposition

Prometheus Capital Partners works with investee companies over a multiyear investment horizon to maximize shareholder value by:

1. Contributing fresh equity capital

 

2. Applying the executive operating experience of the Prometheus partners, including down-cycle management, for:

 

  • Strategic plan development and implementation

  • Financial management

  • Recruitment of needed managers

     

3. Providing access to key decision makers at top Russian banks, and working with management to restructure outstanding debts

 

4. Drawing on the partners’ extensive experience in M&A and public capital markets to ensure both growth and a profitable exit
